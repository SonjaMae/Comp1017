Top 3 Most Popular Video Games

Minecraft

Minecraft is a game developed by Markus Persson of Sweden. It is a sandbox survival game which has sold over 180 million copies.

Fortnite

Fortnite is a game developed by Epic Games, an American company. It has multiple game modes but is best known for its battle royale mode. Grand Theft Auto 5 Grand Theft Auto 5 is a game developed by the American company Rockstar Games. It is an open world story driven game with a strong multiplayer component.
